Top three activities to do at Lima

Park El Olivar

The parque olivar is an oasis from the busy, traffic-filled city. The 400+ year old olive trees provide plenty of shade around the meandering paths. This is definitely a lovely spot in San Isidro.

Museum of Peru

The Royal Tombs of Sipán Museum (Spanish: Museo Tumbas Reales de Sipán) is a museum in Lima. It contains most of the important artifacts found at Huaca Rajada by archeologist Walter Alva in 1987.

Gastronomy

Enjoy memorable experiences, because if there is one thing we Peruvians take very seriously, it is food!
